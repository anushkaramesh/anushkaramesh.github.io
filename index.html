About Me

Hi, I'm Anushka Ramesh

MS in Business Analytics Student at University of Massachusetts Amherst

I’m currently a graduate student at the University of Massachusetts Amherst’s Isenberg School of Management, pursuing a Master of Science in Business Analytics with a concentration in Data Analytics. This program has been an exciting journey that blends my passion for data with practical, real-world business impact.
What excites me most about Business Analytics is its ability to turn raw data into meaningful decisions. Through courses like Business Applications with Python and Statistics for Business, I’ve learned how to uncover patterns, ask the right questions, and transform complex datasets into clear, actionable insights. I’m continually inspired by how thoughtful analysis can shape strategy and drive measurable business growth.
Before joining UMass, I worked as a Packaged App Development Associate at Accenture in Bangalore, supporting a high-traffic SAP Hybris eCommerce platform. There, I gained hands-on experience in data analysis, KPI tracking, and stakeholder collaboration within an enterprise environment. This experience reinforced the importance of data-driven insights and motivated me to further develop my analytical skill set through graduate studies.
I’m currently seeking Summer 2026 internship opportunities where I can apply my analytical skills, contribute to impactful data-driven projects, and continue learning from experienced professionals. I’m especially interested in roles focused on business intelligence, data visualization, and strategic analytics.
